Phishing Scam Targets Remote Workers

Published: August 29, 2025

A recent phishing campaign is targeting remote employees with sophisticated emails that appear to be from their company's IT department. The emails claim that the user's password has expired or that their account has been compromised, urging them to click a link to "verify their information."

Clicking the link leads to a fake login page designed to look identical to the company's real portal. When users enter their credentials, the information is immediately stolen by the attackers. Security experts advise employees to always verify such requests through a different communication channel, such as a phone call to their IT department, before clicking any links.
