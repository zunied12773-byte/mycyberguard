New Ransomware Variant Discovered

Published: August 29, 2025

Cybersecurity researchers have identified a new strain of ransomware, dubbed 'CryptoLock,' that is spreading through a fake software update service. Once installed, the malware encrypts all user files and demands a Bitcoin payment for the decryption key.

Experts warn that this variant is particularly dangerous because it can bypass some traditional antivirus software. To protect against it, users are advised to only download software updates from official, trusted sources and to always keep regular backups of important files on an external hard drive or cloud service.
