Data Breach at a Major Tech Company

Published: August 29, 2025

A major technology company has confirmed a data breach affecting millions of its users. The breach was caused by a misconfigured cloud server that left user data, including names, email addresses, and phone numbers, exposed to the public internet.

The company has since secured the server and is notifying affected users. This incident serves as a reminder of the importance of proper server configuration and the need for companies to regularly audit their security systems to prevent accidental data exposure.
